About Our Host

Isabel is the visionary behind Diplomacy Unscripted, a podcast dedicated to bringing real conversations and diverse perspectives to words on international relations. Her background involves working with foreign agencies, embassies and implementing governance, diplomacy, human rights, and international affairs programs across Africa. This gives her deep insight into regional governance challenges.

The podcast's mission is to unpack diplomacy's role in resolving global conflicts, analyzing both success and challenges. It also explores how technology is changing diplomatic communication and relations in the digital age. Ms. Isabel curates compelling conversations with diplomats, policymakers, civil society, experts, leaders, and citizens.

Ultimately, Diplomacy Unscripted aims to demystify diplomacy, making it accessible and engaging for a broad audience.
