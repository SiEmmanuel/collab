Diplomacy Unscripted

Podcast by Isabel

Diplomacy is often wrapped in layers of formality, jargon, and red tape - but Diplomacy Unscripted peels all that away.Here we humanize diplomacy through bold stories, candid talks, and untold insights - revealing the real people, moments, and missteps behind global negotiations and political headlines. Join Isabel as she uncovers the human stories behind international relations.

About the Host

Isabel is an experienced journalist and foreign policy analyst with over 15 years of experience covering international affairs. She has reported from conflict zones, diplomatic summits, and international organizations across five continents.

Her unique perspective comes from having worked both as a journalist asking tough questions and as a communications director for an international NGO, giving her rare insight into both sides of diplomatic interactions.

When not hosting Diplomacy Unscripted, Isabel teaches a course on media and diplomacy at a leading university and consults for international organizations on strategic communications.
